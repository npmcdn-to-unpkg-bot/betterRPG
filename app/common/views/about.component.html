About the Team!

Have you ever wished that you could make some other use of the time you spend working out? Doing laundry? What about reading a book? What if there was a way to change all those skills you work on in real life and translate them into a character based on you. Something you can adventure with, improve skills, find gear, topple kingdoms with?
What if your desire to be better could be more?
Welcome to Better RPG, our goal is to add a bit of flavor to the every day tasks that you do just to better yourself. There is so much to your daily tasks, so why not log that workout and gain some experience for it? When you got time later, go out into the world and explore!
That's all, no gimmicks. Just Improve yourself, log your efforts and get a reward. Watch yourself improve and grow with us as your daily life becomes the game!
